FAQ

Questions before a first call.

Rightnote is an advisory practice. Each assignment is scoped according to the jurisdiction, market and institutional context.

Is Rightnote a law firm?

No. Rightnote provides strategic and operational advisory. Formal legal opinions or representation should be provided by licensed counsel in the relevant jurisdiction.

Can Rightnote support a CMO or OGC launch?

Yes, at strategy and operations level: governance, membership, repertoire documentation, licensing logic, stakeholder education and implementation sequencing.

Do you work with artists and labels?

Yes, especially when the question connects rights, publishing, release planning, metadata, live economics and international development.

Can training be customized?

Yes. Programs can be designed for artists, managers, labels, public agencies, CMO teams or mixed-sector cohorts.
